Characters:

        This picture contains the members of the 1980 United States Olympic hockey team. Neal Broten (9), is seen skating towards the group of celebrating players. Mark Pavelich (16) is also seen skating towards the group on the far left. In the group of players, Eric Strobel (19) is seen hugging an unnamed player. To the right of the standing players, Bob Suter (20) and Mark Wells (15) are tackling a pile of players on the ground. Lying on his back on the right of the picture is Mike Ramsey (5). Players not shown in this picture include: Steve Janaszak (1), Ken Morrow (3), Bill Baker (6), the alternate captain, David Silk (8), Mark Johnson (10), who scored the second goal with one second left in the period and the third goal, Steve Christoff (11), Mike Eruzione (21), the captain and scored the game winning goal, Dave Christian (23), Rob McClanahan (24), Buzz Schneider (25), who scored the first goal, Phil Verchota (27), John Harrington (28), who assisted Eruzione for the game winning goal, and Jim Craig (30), who was the starting goalie and stopped 36 out of 39 shots (Benree;citation).
