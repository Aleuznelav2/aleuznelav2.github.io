Characters:

        This picture contains the members of the 1980 United States Olympic hockey team. Neal Broten (9), is seen skating towards the group of celebrating players. Mark Pavelich (16) is also seen skating towards the group on the far left. In the group of player, Eric Strobel (19) is seen hugging an unnamed player. To the right of the standing players, Bob Suter (20) and Mark Wells (15) are tackling a pile of players on the ground. Lying on his back on the right of the picture is Mike Ramsey (5). Players not shown in this picture include: Steve Janaszak (1), Ken Morrow (3), Bill Baker (6), the alternate captain, David Silk (8), Mark Johnson (10), Steve Christoff (11), Mike Eruzione (21), the captain, Dave Christian (23), Rob McClanahan (24), Buzz Schneider (25), Phil Verchota (27), John Harrington (28), and Jim Craig (30) (Benree). The head coach of the team was Herb Brooks, and his brutal conditioning sessions and tough coaching attitude allowed the Americans to keep up with the Soviets throughout the game and allowed them to come out on top in the end (“The 1980 Miracle”).
