Lasting Impact:

lozii The photo was voted as Sports Illustrated’s most iconic magazine cover of all time which signifies its importance in sports history (citation). These Olympics took place in the midst of the Cold War, and the victory over the U.S.S.R. was a great morale booster for the American public. It was more than an Olympic upset, it was an ideologically victory. The United States was starting to struggle economically, and the victory gave millions of Americans a reason to celebrate (history.com citation). It gave Americans a new sense of nationalism, as the win was “as meaningful as the Berlin Airlift or the Apollo moon landing” (history.com citation). Many Hollywood films were created based off of the Miracle on Ice, most notably the film Miracle produced by Walt Disney Pictures in 2004 (citation).
